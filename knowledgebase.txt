*** HOSTEL OFFICIAL RULEBOOK ***

1. GENERAL RULES:
   - Quiet Hours: 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM. No loud music.
   - Visitors: Allowed in the lobby ONLY between 4:00 PM and 7:00 PM. No overnight guests.
   - Alcohol/Smoking: Strictly prohibited inside hostel premises. Fine: Rs. 5000.
   - ID Card: Must be worn at all times in common areas.

2. GATE TIMINGS:
   - Main Gate Opens: 6:00 AM
   - Main Gate Closes: 10:00 PM (Strictly)
   - Late Entry: Requires prior approval from Warden via the "Complaints" tab.

3. WIFI DETAILS:
   - Network Name: "Hostel_Student_5G"
   - Password: "SecureHostel2025"
   - Usage Limit: Educational purposes only. Torrenting is blocked.

4. ROOM POLICIES:
   - Room Cleaning: Every Saturday morning by housekeeping staff.
   - Damage Charges: Any damage to hostel property will be charged to the student.
   - Room Inspection: Monthly inspection by warden on the 1st of every month.
   - AC Usage: Allowed only between 9:00 PM and 6:00 AM to save electricity.

5. PAYMENT POLICIES:
   - Rent Payment: Due on the 5th of every month. Late fee: Rs. 100 per day after the 10th.
   - Mess Charges: Included in monthly rent. No refunds for missed meals.
   - Security Deposit: Rs. 5000 (Refundable at checkout with room inspection).

6. LAUNDRY SERVICES:
   - Available Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
   - Cost: Rs. 300 per month for unlimited service.
   - Pickup and Delivery: Every Monday and Thursday.

*** FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ***

Q: Can I bring my laptop/desktop?
A: Yes, personal electronics are allowed. Please register them with the office.

Q: Is parking available?
A: Yes, free parking for bikes. Car parking: Rs. 500/month.

Q: Can I cook in my room?
A: No, cooking is not allowed in rooms. Use the common kitchen on the ground floor.

Q: What if I lose my room key?
A: Report to the office immediately. Replacement key cost: Rs. 200.

Q: Can I change my room?
A: Room changes are allowed only in special circumstances. Submit a request to the warden.

Q: What are the checkout procedures?
A: Give 15 days notice, clear all dues, return room keys, and schedule a room inspection.
